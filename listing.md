The ClearMetric Stock Options Calculator is a **professional Excel spreadsheet** that helps tech workers understand and plan their stock option exercises — whether you have ISO (Incentive Stock Options) or NSO (Non-Qualified Stock Options).

**Sheet 1: Options Calculator**
